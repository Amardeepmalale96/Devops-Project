What is Healthcare?

Healthcare is the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease, illness, injury, and other physical and mental impairments in humans. It is delivered by practitioners in various fields like medicine, nursing, pharmacy, and more.

Importance of Healthcare

The healthcare industry is crucial to individuals worldwide, playing a significant role in improving the quality of life. It includes various players like hospitals, doctors, and diagnostic labs.

Classification of Healthcare Industry

The modern healthcare sector is divided into sub-sectors like:

  • Hospital Activities
  • Medical and Dental Practice
  • Other Human Health Activities (physiotherapy, pathology clinics, etc.)

Delivery of Healthcare Services

Healthcare services are delivered in various ways, including face-to-face consultations or remote options like telehealth through video calls or messaging.

Medical Tourism

Medical tourism involves traveling across borders to obtain healthcare services, often for elective or specialized surgeries. Over 50 countries have identified medical tourism as a national industry.
